# ParkSense AI: Hackathon Judge Guide

Welcome to the **ParkSense AI** platform! 

## The Problem
Cities generate millions of parking violation tickets, but treat them merely as revenue. They fail to realize that this data contains the exact coordinates of future traffic gridlocks.

## The Solution
ParkSense AI is an intelligent data foundation that transforms messy ticket data into a **Predictive Smart City Operating System**.

## How to Evaluate This Project

We recommend walking through the platform in the following order to understand the "Chain of Intelligence":

### 1. Descriptive Intelligence
Look at the **CSI™ (Congestion Severity Index)**. We don't just count tickets. We calculate the physical width of the road lost, the impact on traffic speed, and proximity to hospitals. 

### 2. Predictive Intelligence
Instead of reacting, the platform forecasts. Check the **1h and 24h Risk Forecasts**. We use Random Forest ML models to warn authorities *before* a road blocks.

### 3. Prescriptive Intelligence
Look at the **Digital Twin**. What happens if we tow 50% of the cars? The physics engine simulates the exact ROI and traffic delay hours saved.

### 4. Operational Intelligence
Look at the **Smart Enforcement Planner (SEP)**. The AI assumes the police only have 5 tow trucks today. It uses Traveling Salesperson optimization to generate the perfect daily patrol route.

### 5. Executive Intelligence
Try the **Smart City AI Copilot (SCAC™)**. Ask it: "Which hotspot should we target first?" It will use RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) to read the backend metrics and give you an evidence-backed answer.

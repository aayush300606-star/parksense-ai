# Hotspot DNA Profiling Report

**Objective:** Create a unique behavioral and prescriptive signature for every single traffic hotspot.

## What is Hotspot DNA?
A Hotspot DNA string is a concatenated signature combining the `Root Cause`, the `Temporal Predictability`, and the `Intervention Priority`.

*Example Signature:* `RC_METRO::High::Critical`

## Anatomy of the DNA
1. **The Primary Cause:** The deterministic driver of the congestion (e.g., Metro Commuters).
2. **The Secondary Cause:** The compounding factor (e.g., Ride-Hailing drops occurring at the same location).
3. **The Peak Violation Time:** The exact hour the behavior peaks (e.g., 17:00).
4. **Predictability:** "High" means the variance is low; the violations happen at the same time every day. "Low" means the violations are sporadic and difficult to police.
5. **Intervention Priority:** The priority assigned by the engine based on the expected physical capacity recovery.

**Conclusion:** This DNA structure allows the AI Copilot to instantly understand the complex behavioral mechanics of any location on the map.

# Policy Impact Simulation Report

**Objective:** Mathematically quantify the benefit of long-term urban design changes.

## The Policy Impact Engine
Before a city signs a contract to rebuild a curbside or install automated bollards, they need to justify the budget. The Policy Impact Engine leverages the `Digital Twin` to run long-term simulations on infrastructure changes.

### Metrics Generated
If a "Dedicated Loading Bay" policy is simulated for a commercial hotspot, the engine outputs:
1. **Capacity Recovery:** (e.g., +1.5 Lanes recovered).
2. **CSI Improvement:** (e.g., Permanent 35-point reduction in Congestion Severity).
3. **Economic (PIS) Recovery:** (e.g., A 35% reduction in daily Rupee loss due to commuter delay).

**Conclusion:** City planners can now present data-backed ROI arguments to municipal councils when requesting funding for infrastructure projects.

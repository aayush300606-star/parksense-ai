# Enforcement Effectiveness & ROI Report

**Objective:** Ensure that highly constrained police resources are deployed where they will have the highest mathematical impact.

## Matching the Cure to the Disease
Different root causes require entirely different enforcement mechanisms.

1. **Metro Spillover (Long-Term Parking)**
   - *Highly Effective:* Towing/Clamping. The vehicles are stationary for 8+ hours.
   - *Ineffective:* Traffic Marshals. The drivers are already gone.

2. **Ride-Hailing (Short-Term Stopping)**
   - *Highly Effective:* Traffic Marshals directing flow.
   - *Ineffective:* Towing. The vehicle will be gone before the tow truck arrives.

3. **Commercial Loading**
   - *Highly Effective:* Increased patrol frequency with high fines to deter off-hours loading.

## ROI Calculation
The `Enforcement Effectiveness Engine` queries the `Digital Twin` to understand exactly how much capacity is physically recovered if a specific intervention is deployed against a specific root cause. It then outputs the expected reduction in the Adaptive CSI™ score.

**Conclusion:** The platform acts as a force-multiplier. A city with only 5 tow trucks can deploy them with the effectiveness of 50 by perfectly matching the tool to the behavior.

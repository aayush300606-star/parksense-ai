# Judge Walkthrough Mode (Execution Strategy)

To ensure maximum impact during a 2-minute hackathon pitch, we have structured the platform to follow a perfect "Problem-to-Solution" narrative flow.

## The 2-Minute Flow

1. **The Problem (Descriptive)**
   - *"Look at this map. This isn't just a cluster of parking tickets; this is a physical blockade on the city's arteries."*
   - Showcase the **CSI™** score and the **PIS™** (Economic Loss in Rupees).

2. **The Future (Predictive)**
   - *"We don't wait for the traffic jam to happen."*
   - Showcase the Prediction Engine forecasting an 85% risk for tomorrow. Open the "Why?" (SHAP) panel to prove the model's logic.

3. **The Solution (Prescriptive)**
   - *"What should the city do? Let's ask the Digital Twin."*
   - Hit "Run Simulation". Show how towing the vehicles recovers exactly 1.5 lanes of effective width.

4. **The Execution (Operational)**
   - *"The city only has 5 tow trucks today. Where do they go?"*
   - Show the Smart Enforcement Planner (SEP). The optimization engine has already dispatched Team Alpha to the critical hotspot to execute the towing scenario before the peak hour hits.

5. **The Executive Layer (AI Copilot)**
   - *"And the mayor doesn't need to read any charts."*
   - Open the SCAC™ Copilot and ask, "What is our top priority today?" The RAG system answers in plain English, citing the CSI and Simulation data.

This flow guarantees complete understanding of the technical depth and business value in under 120 seconds.

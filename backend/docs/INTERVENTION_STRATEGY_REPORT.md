# Strategic Intervention Recommendation Report

**Objective:** Provide actionable, multi-horizon operational plans to city planners.

## The Three Horizons of Intervention
The `Intervention Recommendation Engine` does not stop at telling the police to go issue a fine. It generates a full lifecycle strategy.

### 1. Immediate Action (Today)
What can be done in the next 60 minutes?
- *Example:* Dispatch rapid-response Tow Trucks to clear an arterial blockage.

### 2. Short-Term Action (This Month)
What can be done to deter the behavior?
- *Example:* Alter the routing of standard police patrols to pass through the hotspot 3 times per day.
- *Example:* Install temporary plastic barricades to prevent mounting the curb.

### 3. Long-Term Infrastructure Fix (This Year)
How do we permanently solve the root cause?
- *Example:* If the root cause is Ride-Hailing outside a hospital, the long-term fix is to re-architect the curbside to establish a legalized, geofenced "Pickup/Drop-off Zone."

**Conclusion:** This module transforms ParkSense from a tool used purely by the Traffic Police into an urban planning tool used by the Mayor's Office.
